feature

heavy weights

heavy weights increase the power and strength of your muscles without significantly adding bulk or size, especially for women. This means that everyday physical tasks get easier, and consistent training will increase the amount of weight you can lift. You'll look stronger, too.

yoga classes

yoga classes at the gym are cheaper. This is particularly true if you already have a gym membership, or if you want to take other fitness classes and have access to amenities such a weight room or pool. Note that some gyms do charge a premium for yoga classes, so make sure to clarify this point.

general
